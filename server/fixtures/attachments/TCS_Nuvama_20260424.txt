NUVAMA INSTITUTIONAL EQUITIES · INDIA IT SERVICES
TCS · Discretionary deal deferrals intensify; downgrade to Sell

We downgrade TCS to Sell with a revised 12-month price target of ₹3,400 (prior ₹3,800)
ahead of the 4QFY26 result. Discretionary deal deferrals are intensifying across BFSI
and hi-tech verticals on our channel work, and we see limited scope for a utilization-led
margin rescue.

Key observations:
- BFSI vertical showing clear deferral signals in 4QFY26 based on checks with deal
  advisors; we model ~8% revenue cut on the BFSI run-rate into FY27
- Hi-tech vertical guidance cuts look likely as client restructuring decisions extend
  into 1HFY27
- Utilisation leverage is exhausted; any NIM-style margin cushion is gone by FY27 exit
- GenAI attach looks bundled into existing deals; we don't see incremental revenue

Risks: deal wins reaccelerate in 1HFY27 (scenario we price at 25% probability), in which
case the downgrade would be premature.
